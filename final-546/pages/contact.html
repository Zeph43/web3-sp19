FAQ

Depending on availability and shipping restrictions, purchases may be made either directly through gucci.com or through the assistance of our online personal shoppers. You can contact us at 877-482-2430 or via email at assistance@us-onlineshopping.chameleon.com. Voicemails and emails will be responded to within 1-2 business days. Please note we are available Monday-Friday from 8AM-10PM(EST) and Saturday 9AM-10PM(EST), excluding holidays.
Deliveries are during office hours, Tuesday to Saturday for packages shipped with Ground service and Monday to Friday for packages shipped with 2nd Day or Overnight service. As the courier will require a signature, we suggest you choose a shipping address where you or someone you trust is available during the day. In the event no one is available to sign for the package, FedEx will leave a notification and a contact number. If you require assistance or are unable to contact the courier, do not hesitate to contact us.
Preorders and Backorders (except personalized or made-to-order) that have not yet shipped can be cancelled by contacting 877-482-2430 or via email at assistance@us-onlineshopping.chameleon.com, as soon as possible. Orders for items immediately available cannot be cancelled or modified once they have been successfully submitted. We advise you to follow the return instructions included in your package, or refuse the shipment upon delivery, and it will be returned to our attention. Please allow 7 to 10 business days for your refund to be processed once it arrives to our warehouse. Made to order items and personalized items are considered final sale and cannot be returned to chameleon.com for a refund. Once a Made to Order has been confirmed, it cannot be cancelled.
